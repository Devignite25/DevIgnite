Privacy Policy

OneDay Journal

Last Updated: November 8, 2025
Your Privacy Matters

OneDay Journal is built with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

1. Data Storage

All your journal entries, photos, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect your personal information
  • Transmit your data to external servers
  • Store your entries in the cloud
  • Share your information with third parties

Your journal is yours and yours alone.

2. Authentication & Security

PIN Security

Your PIN is securely hashed using SHA-256 encryption before being stored in encrypted storage. We never store your PIN in plain text.

Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition), this data is managed entirely by your device's secure enclave. Biometric data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the app or any third party
  • Is managed by your device's operating system

3. Photos & Media

Photos you attach to journal entries are:

  • Stored in your app's private directory
  • Not accessible to other apps
  • Compressed to save storage space
  • Deleted when you delete the associated entry

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos for journal entries (optional)
  • Storage: To save and retrieve your journal entries and photos
  • Notifications: To send daily reminders (optional, can be disabled)
  • Biometrics: For secure authentication using fingerprint/face recognition (optional)

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and are never used to collect data about you.

5. Data Export & Backup

You can export your data at any time using the backup feature in Settings. Exported data:

  • Is in JSON format
  • Includes your journal entries and metadata
  • Does NOT include actual image files (only paths)
  • Is saved to your device's download folder or shared via your choice of app

Important: You are responsible for backing up your data. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be permanently deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

OneDay Journal does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking tools
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media integrations

The app operates entirely offline and does not connect to external services.

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Individual Entries: Delete any entry at any time
  • All Data: Use "Delete All Entries" in Settings to permanently remove all data
  • App Uninstall: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data

Data deletion is immediate and irreversible.

8. Children's Privacy

OneDay Journal does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for personal use and does not require age verification.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
