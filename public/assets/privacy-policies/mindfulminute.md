# Privacy Policy for MindfulMinute

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction

DEVIGNITE LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MindfulMinute mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**MindfulMinute collects ZERO user data.** We are committed to protecting your privacy. All information generated or stored by the App remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information
- Device information
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Location data
- Crash reports
- Any other user data

## Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

### Audio Recordings
- **Personal Affirmations**: Audio recordings you create using the microphone
- **Custom Sounds**: Audio files you upload or download
- **Storage Location**: Device local storage
- **Access**: Only you can access these files through the App

### App Settings
- **PIN Code**: Stored encrypted in secure device storage
- **Biometric Data**: Handled by your device's native authentication (we never access the actual biometric data)
- **App Preferences**: Breathing patterns, notification times, volume settings
- **Storage**: Encrypted local storage using expo-secure-store

### Cached Data
- **Pixabay Search Results**: Temporarily cached during audio search
- **Duration**: Cleared when app closes or cache expires

## Permissions Requested

The App requests the following permissions:

### Required Permissions
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: To restore notification reminders after device restart
- **VIBRATE**: To provide haptic feedback during interactions
- **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM**: To deliver notification reminders at precise times
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: To send daily reminder notifications

### Optional Permissions
You can use the App without granting these permissions:

- **RECORD_AUDIO**: Only if you want to record personal affirmations
  - **When requested**: When you tap the microphone button
  - **Usage**: Audio is recorded and saved locally on your device
  - **Never transmitted**: Recordings never leave your device

- **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE**: Only if you want to upload custom audio files
  - **When requested**: When you upload audio from your device
  - **Usage**: To access and save audio files
  - **Never transmitted**: Files remain on your device

## Biometric Authentication

If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition):
- **Handled by device**: Your device's native authentication system handles all biometric data
- **We never access**: The App never accesses your actual biometric data
- **Device-level security**: All biometric processing happens at the device level

## Third-Party Services

### Pixabay API (Optional)
If you use the audio search feature:
- **What it does**: Searches Pixabay for free audio files
- **Data sent**: Only your search query
- **Pixabay Privacy Policy**: https://pixabay.com/service/privacy/
- **Optional**: You can use the App without searching Pixabay

### No Other Third Parties
We do not use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting services

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit any user data because:
1. We don't collect any user data
2. All data stays on your device
3. No network communication for user data

## Children's Privacy

MindfulMinute does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data at all, the App is safe for all ages.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App, please note that no personal information has been collected or transmitted.

## Data Security

### Local Security Measures
- **Encrypted Storage**: Sensitive data (PIN) is encrypted using expo-secure-store
- **Device-Level Protection**: Data is protected by your device's security features
- **No Cloud Sync**: Data never leaves your device

### Your Responsibility
- Set a strong PIN code
- Enable biometric authentication if available
- Keep your device secure
- Use device encryption if available

## Data Retention and Deletion

### Retention
- Data is stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it

### Deletion
To delete all app data:

**Android:**
1. Go to Settings → Apps → MindfulMinute
2. Tap "Storage"
3. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
4. Confirm deletion

**Or uninstall the app:**
1. Long-press the app icon
2. Tap "Uninstall"
3. Confirm

**Note:** Deleting app data cannot be undone. All recordings, settings, and configurations will be permanently erased from your device.

- **Deletion**: You can delete all data at any time through device settings
